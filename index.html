Hello, there! My name is Kirsten Green and I am Visual Designer based in Washington, DC.

Flush

Flush - When I was a kid, I remember spending countless evenings sitting around the table with family trying to win a game of Crazy Eights or Hearts. It was these memories that really inspired the creation of this playing card game brand.

Garamond

Garamond - After taking a design history course, I found myself particularly interested in the origins of type design. When given the opportunity to write about a typeface, I quickly dove into the history of Garamond by Claude Garamond, who is one of the founding fathers of this process.

Letter Color Associations

Letter Color Associations - Inspired by synesthesia, a neurological phenomenon that often connects two or more sensory experiences, I wanted to see if people unconsciously associated certain colors with letters in the English alphabet.

Motion Graphics

Motion Graphics - This collection of projects takes traditionally static media and brings them to life by conveying meaning through the use of motion.
